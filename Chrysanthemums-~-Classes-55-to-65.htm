Open Classes 55 to 65: Chrysanthemums

Please supply your own vases.
Except where otherwise stated, Vases may comprise one or more cultivars.

The "B.S. Palmer" Challenge Cup will be awarded to the exhibitor gaining the highest number of points in this section, Classes 55 to 65.

The "National Chrysanthemum Society" Silver Medal and Certificate will be awarded at the judges' discretion to the best exhibits in this section - Classes 55 to 65.

Classes 55 to 62 are for disbudded cultivars. Classes 63 to 65 are for sprays.
