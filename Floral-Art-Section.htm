Floral Art Section

82nd ANNUAL SHOW

The Floral Art Classes are open to all. Drapes and decorative material may be used within the 75cms allotted bench length.

Entries close at 9pm on Saturday, 26th August 2017 - see Show Rule 1

Staging Times  Sunday, 27th August between 2pm and 5pm, and Monday
28th August between 8am and 10.15am

Judging commences at 10.30am.  No entry may be removed before 5pm

Prize Money  All Floral Art classes:  1st Prize - £5.00, 2nd Prize - £3.00, 3rd Prize - £2.00

The following Awards will be made to winners at 4.30pm at the Show Secretary’s Tent:

The Maidenhead Advertiser Challenge Bowl presented to the exhibitor gaining most points in the Floral Art Section.  In the event of a tie, the Challenge Bowl will be awarded to the exhibitor with awards of higher merit.  The exhibitor who won the Bowl last year will not be eligible to win the trophy this year.
‘Mr Sleepy’ Cup for Best in Show, and the Horsham Cup for the runner-up
Novice Spoon for the winner of Class 6 - Donated by The Littlewick Green Show Society.  The Novice Class is open only to those who have not gained a 1st Prize previously at this Show.  

The Theme for this year:
FLORAL ARRANGEMENTS INSPIRED BY COLOURS  

Only natural plant material is to be used. Fresh flowers and foliage must predominate and accessories may be used.

Class 1
My Favourite Colour

Class 2
Whiter Than White

Class 3
Autumn Gold

Class 4
Not All Leaves Are Green (a design using fresh foliage only)

Class 5
A miniature arrangement not to exceed 15cms (6”) wide, deep or high.

Novice Class 6
A hand-tied bouquet for a bridesmaid, staged in an up-right position, in water.
