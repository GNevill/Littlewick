Main Ring 1

Starts 08:30hrs

Clear Round Jumping

Jumps up to 45cm. Entries at ringside £5. No pre-payment taken.

Starts 09:00hrs
Jackets must be worn

Class 1: Starters Jumping

Riders under 16 years old. Jumps up to 45cm in the first round. May be led.

Class 2: Minimus Jumping

Jumps up to 60cm in the first round. Riders entering this class may not take part in any other main ring classes, apart from classes 3 and 4. Cup for best turned out pony and rider.

Class 3: Novice Jumping (Two Phase)

Horses/ponies not to have been placed first in any jumping class before the day. Jumps up to 70cm in the first round.


Class 4: Horse and Hound

Horses/ponies/handlers & dogs to jump in relay against the clock.
Fences up to 45cm. Dogs may compete on or off the lead. Prize for best co-ordinated outfits!

To follow, if time until 14:00

Clear Round Jumping

Jumps up to 80cm. Entries at ringside £5. No pre-payment taken.

Class 5: Top Score

Choose your own course to collect points. Jumps 60cm-1m.

Class 6: Local Jumping (Two Phase)

Horses/ponies kept within a 15 mile radius of the showground.
Jumps approximately 80cm in first round.
Cup: Brookhart Cup

Class 7: Pairs Relay

Jumps approximately 80cm.
Class judged on time. (No jump off)

Class 8: Open Intermediate Jumping (Two Phase)

Jumps approximately 85cm in the first round.
Cup: Committee trophy

Two Phase:
If the competitor completes a clear round they will continue to jump off against the clock immediately.

Please bring your own water for your horses, ponies and dogs.
Please ensure all your rubbish is taken home.
Thank you.
