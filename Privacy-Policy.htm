Littlewick Green Show Society

Privacy Policy

  1. Littlewick Green Show Society run the Littlewick Show, a country show with a Horticultural tent, Horse show, Dog show, Classic Car show, funfair etc. with trade and charity stands in the locality of Maidenhead in the United Kingdom.
  2. This website gives visitors to the show details of events and attractions at the show and provides persons wishing to enter the various parts of the show.
  3. Littlewick Show uses the data collected on this website to remind entrants to previous shows of the upcoming Show.
  4. We will look after personal information securely and we will follow the data protection legislation. We will not give personal information about you or any personal information you may provide on other people to anyone else or another organisation unless we have to by law.
  5. The lawful basis to collect the information in this form is that it is necessary to update entrants of the rules and classes so that they can enter Show events.
  6. Some or all of the following are the types of data collected via this website –
    1. last name, first name
    2. personal address
    3. personal email address
    4. personal phone number
    5. date of last entry in show
  7. Personal data is deleted five years after your last entry in the Show or your last request for a schedule or information about the show.
  8. You have the right to see, modify and delete the information that we hold on you. If you wish to contact us about the storage of personal data please contact the Show Sectretary, Valerie Bosley at 18, Pinkneys Road, Maidenhead, SL6 5DH.
  9. Littlewick Show will only allow trained staff to process the data and will ensure that the data is kept in a secure environment.
  10. Littlewick Show will not use subcontractors in the processing of the data.
