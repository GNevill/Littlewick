Open Classes

Top Tray

This class is for three vegetables chosen from the following seven, all being displayed on a tray not exceeding 18ins. (45.5 cms) by 12 ins. (30.5 cms) and all vegetables must be displayed within the confines of the tray.

The tray may be dressed but only parsley may be used.

  1. Three Potatoes
  2. Three carrots, without top foliage.
  3. Three Tomatoes.
  4. Six Pods Peas.
  5. Six Runner Beans.
  6. Three Beetroot
  7. Three Onions
