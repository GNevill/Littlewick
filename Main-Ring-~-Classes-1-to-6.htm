Main Ring 1

Starts 08:30hrs

Clear Round Jumping

Jumps up to 2ft. Entries at ringside £5. No pre-payment taken.

Starts 09:00hrs
Jackets must be worn

Class 1: Minimus Jumping

Combination of rider and horse/pony not to have been placed 1st, 2nd or 3rd in any jumping event. Jumps approximately 2ft in the first round.

Riders entering this class may not take part in any other main ring classes, apart from classes 2 and 3. Cup for best turned out pony and rider

Class 2: Novice Jumping (Two Phase)

Horses/ponies not to have won more than £10 in total in jumping classes.
Jumps approximately 2ft 3ins in the first round.

Class 3: Horse and Hound

Horses/ponies/handlers & dogs to jump in relay against the clock.
Fences up to 1ft 6ins.
Dogs may compete on or off the lead.

To follow, for 30 minutes

Clear Round Jumping

Jumps up to 2ft 6ins. Entries at ringside £5. No pre-payment taken.

Class 4: Local Jumping (Two Phase)

Horses/ponies kept within a 15 mile radius of the showground.
Jumps approximately 2ft 6ins in first round.
Cup: Brookhart Cup

Class 5: Pairs Relay

Jumps approximately 2ft 6ins.
Class judged on time. (No jump off)

Class 6: Open Intermediate Jumping (Two Phase)

Jumps approximately 2ft 9ins in the first round.
Cup: Committee trophy

Two Phase:
If the competitor completes a clear round they will continue to jump off against the clock immediately.

Please bring your own water for your horses, ponies and dogs.
Please ensure all your rubbish is taken home.
Thank you.
