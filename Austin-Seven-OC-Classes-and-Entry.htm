Littlewick Show 2017

BOTTLE MEADOW, KNOWL HILL, RG10 9YJ
BANK HOLIDAY MONDAY, 28th AUGUST 2017



One of the biggest one day shows in the South with fun for all the family.
The show starts at 10 a.m. Exhibits to be parked by 9:45 a.m.

CLASSES:

A. Austin 7s up to 1930
B. Austin 7s 1931-35 (Chrome Radiators)
C. Austin 7s 1934-39 (Painted Radiators)
D. Austin 7 Specials – All years
E. Vintage Austin register vehicles
F. Veteran & Vintage Cars to 1955
G. Post-Vintage Cars 1956 - 1987
H. Motorcycles up to 1987
J. Light Commercial and Military up to 3.5 tons
K. American Cars post WW2 up to 1987

We regret that we are unable to accept steam powered entries this year due to insurance restrictions.

ENTERING YOUR VEHICLE

Entries are subject to our Terms and Conditions which can be seen here.
We prefer entries to be made and paid for online as it reduces the administration work and you will be able to print off your entry number as soon as you have registered and paid.
Enter online for £3 at http://thamesvalleyclassiccars.org.uk/LittlewickShow
