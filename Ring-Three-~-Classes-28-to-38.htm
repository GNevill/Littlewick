Ring Three

Starts 09:00hrs
Please state breeds on entry form for Mountain & Moorland classes

Class 28: Mountain & Moorland: In Hand - Small Breeds
Welsh Sec A & B, Shetland, Dartmoor, Exmoor.
Open to registered and unregistered breeds of any age or sex.

Class 29: Mountain & Moorland: In Hand - Large Breeds
Welsh Sec C & D, Dales, Connemara, New Forest, Fell, Highland
Open to registered and unregistered breeds of any age or sex.

Class 30: Coloured In Hand
Piebald, skewbald, tri-colour, palomino, dun, roan, cream or spotted. Open to horses/ponies any height or sex.

Not before 11:00hrs

Class 31: Veterans In Hand
Horses and ponies 17 years and over.

Class 32: Lead Rein
Open to any rider/horse/pony. Combination may not enter Class 11 or 13.

Not before 12:30hrs

Class 33: Mountain & Moorland: Ridden - Small Breeds
Welsh Sec A & B, Shetland, Dartmoor, Exmoor.
Open to registered and unregistered breeds of any age and sex.

Class 34: Mountain & Moorland: Ridden - Large Breeds
Welsh Sec C & D, Dales, Connemara, New Forest, Fell, Highland
Open to registered and unregistered breeds of any age or sex.

Class 35: Ridden Veteran
Horses/ponies 17 years old and over. No martingales or bandages/boots.
Cup: Bunce Cup

Class 36: Race Horse to Riding Horse

Class 37: Riding Club Horse
For horses exceeding 14.2hh. Judged on suitability for Riding Club activities. Snaffle bridles only. No boots, bandages or martingales. May be required to jump a small fence.
Cup: Cada Toys Challenge Trophy

Class 38: Riding Horse
For horses 15.2hh and over. Horses will not be required to jump.
Cup: Battledore Challenge Trophy

Please bring your own water for your horses, ponies and dogs.
Please ensure all your rubbish is taken home.
Thank you.
