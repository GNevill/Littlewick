Ring Two

Starts 09:00hrs

Class 7: Best Condition

In Hand. Horses and ponies of any size or age.

Class 8: Tack and Turnout

Section A Show turnout (must be plaited)
Section B Hacking turnout
Cup: The Vigilante Challenge Cup

Class 9: First Pony

Ponies any height. Children 10 years and under on date of show.
To be judged on condition, presentation, turnout and character of pony.
Children entering this class may not enter Classes 11 or 29.

Class 10: Coloured In Hand

Piebald, skewbald, tri-colour or spotted. Open to horses/ponies any height or sex


Not before 11:00hrs

Class 11: Second Pony

Ponies any height, suitable for all pony club activities.
Children 10 years and over on date of show.
To be judged on condition, presentation, turnout and rider.

Competitors may not enter Classes 9 or 29.

Class 12: Ridden Cob

Open to horses 14.2hh (148cm) or over. Horse may be ridden by judge.
Horses need not be hogged.

Class 13: Ridden Coloured

Piebald, skewbald, tri-colour or spotted. Open to horses/ponies any height or sex


Not before 13:00hrs

Class 14: Fancy Dress

Open to riders of any age. Horses/ponies may be ridden.
Riders must wear standard hats and suitable riding boots.
Equines must wear saddles if ridden.

Class 15: Foreign Breeds Ridden

Open to any foreign breed except warmbloods.
Traditional (see rule 9) or non-traditional dress.
Class will be split at Judge's discretion.

Please state breed on entry form.

Class 16: Handy Pony

Open to any rider/horse combination.
To complete a course of obstacles and tasks, to include one or more very small jumps.

Please bring your own water for your horses, ponies and dogs.
Please ensure all your rubbish is taken home.
Thank you.
